Welcome to IKEA Furniture Info!
IKEA is a global, Swedish-founded retailer recognized as the world's largest furniture seller, specializing in affordable, Scandinavian-designed, flat-pack furniture. 
Founded in 1943 by Ingvar Kamprad, the company focuses on functional home furnishings, operating hundreds of stores worldwide with a mission to create a better everyday life for many people. 
Key Information about IKEA:
History & Name: Founded in 1943, the name is an acronym for founder Ingvar Kamprad, his family farm Elmtaryd, and his hometown Agunnaryd.
Business Model: Known for "flat-pack" design, which lowers shipping and storage costs, allowing for lower retail prices.
Presence: As of 2025, there are over 480 IKEA stores operating in 63 countries.
Ownership: Operated by the Inter IKEA Group and Ingka Group, with headquarters in the Netherlands.
The company is known for its immersive in-store experience, which includes showrooms, restaurants serving Swedish food, and a focus on self-assembly.
